Our goal is simple; to connect people and plants

Plants Map is a fun, interactive, social site where you can explore gardens, discover plants, and create your own favorite plant collections in your virtual garden. You can also purchase attractive, high-quality, interactive plant tags with QR codes that connect people with plant collections. This allows you to organize your own garden and share it with others.
